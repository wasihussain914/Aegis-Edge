The problem · why we're different

Drones outpace humans. Comms get jammed. “AI that fires” can’t be accredited.

  • Deterministic gate decides in <1 ms — at machine speed, every decision the same every time.
  • A human authorizes every kill — two‑person auth, positive‑ID, fratricide check.
  • The LLM is OFF the kill chain — it explains after the gate decides; it never fires.
  • We don’t compete on a better detector. We compete on a governable, jam‑resilient, cooperative architecture.

Architecture & integration 20%

Edge decides. Cloud explains & remembers.

Edge — Greengrass / Snowball

Deterministic gate + sensor/effector I/O. Runs offline: when the link and cloud are denied, it keeps making shoot / no‑shoot calls locally.

the “EDGE‑AUTONOMOUS” banner in the demo = this boundary, live.

Cloud — GovCloud IL5

Bedrock (threat assessment, async & off the kill chain) · S3 Object‑Lock WORM (signed ledger) · KMS · IAM.

expensive path is bounded & throttled — never on the trigger.
  • SAPIENT / BSI Flex 335 — the open C‑UAS interoperability standard. Partners plug sensors & effectors straight in.
  • Pure, I/O‑free deterministic core → the renderer only draws what the core decides. Clean, testable seams.

Security & feasibility 15%

A deterministic, auditable kill chain is accreditable.

  • Tamper‑evident — hash‑chained Ed25519 signed ledger → S3 Object‑Lock WORM (immutable).
  • Separation of duties — two‑person authorization · positive‑ID · fratricide / blue‑on‑blue check.
  • GovCloud IL5, KMS encryption, IAM least‑privilege · LLM off the kill chain by design.
  • Feasibility superpower: a black box that decides to fire is nearly impossible to accredit — a deterministic gate you can audit line‑by‑line is fieldable.
  • Scales & cost‑bounded: gate is <1 ms; the LLM is async and throttled to narrate a sample — so cost doesn’t grow with threat volume.

Cost & optimization 10%

The money is in sensors & effectors — we're the cheap keystone.

  • Per-site capex — Light (mobile) ~$150–450k · Standard (fixed) ~$0.5–1.5M · Heavy (base) ~$1.5–5M+. Radar / RF / EO-IR + effector dominate; our software is single-digit % of that.
  • Cloud is tiny and FLAT — Bedrock is off the hot path & throttled (~$15–75/mo/site) and doesn't grow with drone count; the deterministic gate is near-free (0.58 µs/decision, measured). Cloud ≈ 1–5% of TCO.
  • Deploy into what exists — AWS Modular Data Center (ruggedized, DDIL-built) under the $9B JWCC vehicle. No infrastructure to build.
  • Capital-light — we sell software + integration per site; the customer/partner owns the hardware. At 100 sites our layer is low single-digit % of TCO.
