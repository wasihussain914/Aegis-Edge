Edge + Cloud — both, by design

Decision runs at the edge on Greengrass, offline & deterministic; Bedrock + audit live in GovCloud and are reached only on reconnect. The split is the architecture.

Clear boundaries & interfaces

Three named contracts (Link-16/CoT, TrackFeatures, pipeline-events) + the hard "LLM-off-kill-chain" boundary, enforced at the IAM layer.

Modular & partner-integrable

The core is a drop-in module — partners swap sensors/effectors at the typed seams and consume the ASFF/CoT ledger downstream. One engine, two units, many missions.

Why this scores Architecture & Integration (20%)

  • Edge + cloud: Greengrass v2 / Snowball edge node decides offline; GovCloud explains + anchors. DDIL-survivable.
  • Boundaries: the kill chain is fully on-edge and never crosses into Bedrock; the audit is immutable (S3 WORM + KMS).
  • Interfaces: Link-16/CoT between units; TrackFeatures into the core; pipeline-events up; ASFF/CoT out — all documented.
  • Modularity: swap sensors, effectors, or the unit — the deterministic spine is unchanged; one engine drives both units.
